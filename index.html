In Memoriam: Rouky Martin

April 18th 1956 - December 30th 2022

Rouky Martin: 66, led a fruitful life until his untimely passing in his home in December 2022.

Born on April 18, 1956 in Newport News, Virginia, Rouky was the oldest of four children born to Rufus Martin and Christine Martin-Heath. Shortly after his birth, his parents moved with him to Brooklyn, New York where he was raised.

Rouky studied computer programming at The City College of New York where he earned his Bachelors Degree in Computer Science. Early on in his career, Rouky went on to work for Telcordia (Bellcore), AT&T and Lucent. He continued to work for several companies as a senior software developer and engineer until retirement. While at Telcordia, Rouky found time to volunteer at the PACE program helping middle and high school students learn about computers on weekends. He also taught as an adjunct professor at Middlesex County College and other online schools.

Besides his love of learning new things and computers, Rouky was a wonderful Dad and greatly loved his children. They were his heart, his pride, and his joy. He was very active in their lives and spent as much quality time with them as possible, taking pictures or video at every chance. Jazz was also a big part of Rouky’s life. He enjoyed attending annual jazz festivals and even traveled to South Africa with the WBGO jazz station in 2019. In retirement, Rouky still found time to substitute teach, try new foods, volunteer in his neighborhood, travel and take long hikes in nature where he took many pictures of animals and flowers.

Rouky was pre-deceased by his parents Christine Martin-Heath, Rufus Martin, step-father Charles Heath, aunt Irene Scott, and uncles John Jr. and David Nathaniel Hayes, as well as his best friend Donald Moore.

Left to cherish Rouky’s memories are his daughters Ayanna, Jamila, and Kaylee Martin of NJ; son Amaad Martin, and step-children Tiburtius "junior" Roberts and Felicidad Hughes (Lance) of NJ; his brothers, Anthony Martin Sr. (Janet) of NY; Clifton "Boobie" Martin (Billie) of CA, and David Martin of NY; step-grandchildren Bryce and Brielle of NJ; nieces La’Cole Martin (Jerome), Ra’Shena Martin, and Anika martin of CA, and Kourtney Martin of NY, and nephew Anthony Jr. (T.J.) martin of NY, and great-nephew Mansa Tatum of ca; uncle William "Freddy" Freddrick Sr. of VA, Yolanda May Taliaferro Hayes, William "Little Man" Freddrick Jr., and John "Jay" Jr. of VA, Tarra Maddocks (Kenny) of PA, and cousins Archie and Allice Harding of NC; cousin Gail of CA, Allan Harding (Hellen) of NM, Lowell Harding of GA, as well as a host of 2nd and 3rd cousins; sister-in-Law Ursula of CA; and special friends, Mr. Kemp of Columbus, GA and Warren Sweeney of Florida.
